Grandmaster Adventurers

These intrepid adventurers are comprised of 10,000 unique NFTs, and have specific, but limited, advantages over other pixel tiles that you might utilize to round out your party. These GMAs, due to their unique and rare natures, often fetch much higher prices on NFT exchanges compared to even epic-rare pixel tiles. With various races, classes, and attributes, there are many different combinations of characteristics that make up GMAs. Their minting price started at 35 ADA, but some rarer race and class combinations with perfect attributes have sold for up to 10,000 ADA! Click here for more information on GMAs.
