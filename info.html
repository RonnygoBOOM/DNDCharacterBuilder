Grandmaster Adventurers

These intrepid adventurers are comprised of 10,000 unique NFTs, and have specific, but limited, advantages over other pixel tiles that you might utilize to round out your party. These GMAs, due to their unique and rare natures, often fetch much higher prices on NFT exchanges compared to even epic-rare pixel tiles. With various races, classes, and attributes, there are many different combinations of characteristics that make up GMAs. Their minting price started at 35 ADA, but some rarer race and class combinations with perfect attributes have sold for up to 10,000 ADA! There is a lot to learn about the nuances of these NFTs, and the floor price is rising daily, so click here for more information on GMAs.

Pixel Tile Adventurers

There are currently 25 adventurer tiles that are not considered Grandmaster Adventurers. These tiles were originally found in purchasable pixel packs, but they are now tradeable on websites like cnft.io for very reasonable prices compared to GMAs. GMAs, however, are far superior because of one balance change that makes them unique: Pixel Tile Adventurers can die permanently, whereas GMAs cannot. When a Pixel Tile Adventurer dies, it leaves behind a tombstone NFT. The rarity of these adventurers is correlated with their combat ability in most cases, so if you are unable to collect a full questing party of GMAs, you may be able to supplement with these cheaper, more temporary tiles. You can collect these adventurers by winning a raffle associated with staking ADA in the DND stake pool, purchasing them on CNFT.io or other websites to come, such as Adapix, or trading with players who currently hold them. The official discord is a great place to find trades and sales as well, without having to go through an NFT exchange website. There are plans for future tile releases with the 2nd set of Pixel Tiles, releasing during the first quarter of 2022

Pixel Tile Accessories

Aside from the quests and adventuring functionality that Grandmaster Adventurers and the lesser Pixel Tile Adventurers may afford, the Inns and Taverns that each player owns are a big part of the game. There are 28 different types of furniture, props, and even a bear rug that can be used to decorate your inn, but these Pixel Tiles are not just for decoration. While many furniture items and props will exist in game, these NFTs can offer additional functionality to your inn, often scaling with rarity. Townspeople, of which there are 3 varieties, may also add some utility to your bar or inn. Perhaps adventuring parties can recover from a difficult quest faster, or earn buffs in an inn by sipping some finer ale from the specialized rare barrels of Pixel Tile 017. Tavern maidens, Pixel Tile 031, may be able to sooth your party's weary spirits with kind words of encouragement, or some innocent flirting. Or perhaps you can recruit more adventurers with a promise of discounted ale if they can slay a Beer Slime, Pixel Tile 032, to replenish your taps.
